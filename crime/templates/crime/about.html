Welcome to JammiGuard

A community-driven crime reporting and monitoring platform designed to enhance safety and security in Kenya. At JammiGuard, we are committed to creating a space where individuals can come together to share and access vital information about crime incidents. Our platform empowers users to report incidents, view crime statistics, and contribute to making our communities safer.


Our Mission

Our mission is clear: to harness the collective strength of the community to combat crime and create safer neighborhoods. We believe that everyone has the right to live in a secure environment, and we're dedicated to achieving that through collaboration and the sharing of critical information.


Privacy and Security

Your safety and privacy are of utmost importance to us. We take stringent measures to protect your personal information and maintain your confidentiality. Our platform is designed with advanced security features to ensure a secure user experience.


Join Us Today

JammiGuard is not just a platform; it's a movement. We invite you to join us in our mission to create safer communities in Kenya. By becoming a part of our community, you're contributing to the greater good and making a real impact on the safety of your neighborhoods.


Contact Us

If you have any questions, suggestions, or feedback, we'd love to hear from you. Feel free to reach out to our dedicated support team via the "Contact Us" page. Your input is invaluable to us as we continue to improve and expand our services.
